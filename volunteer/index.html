Volunteer

Would you like to get involved with Warm Winters?
There are many ways to volunteer!

If you are involved with a ski resort, we can help you set up a Warm Winters chapter there. You can also do a sock drive, toiletry drive, or coat drive at your local school, church or community center. Do you have mad social media skills? You can help us with our social channels. Love to fundraise? We'd love help with that too! Fill out the appropriate application below to get started!
